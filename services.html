HOW WE WORK

Our Onboarding Roadmap

PHASE 1

Discovery & Comprehensive Audit

We catalog all assets, analyze past tax filings for structural drag, inspect current estate wills/trusts, and map out your core income priorities. No strategy is proposed until we have verified your complete financial ecosystem.

PHASE 2

The Aurelius Strategic Blueprint

We deliver an Investment Policy Statement (IPS) specifying customized asset allocations, an integrated tax location map, and structural trust updates. We review these options side-by-side until you approve.

PHASE 3

Seamless Implementation

We coordinate account transfers through major third-party custodians (Schwab or Fidelity). Assets are reallocated with careful attention to tax thresholds, avoiding sudden capital gains exposure.

PHASE 4

Continuous Optimizations

We implement automated tax-loss harvesting, adjust allocations as markets shift, and hold annual review sessions to sync with estate changes or life events.
